Bird Adventure Game

One day, you wake up and surprisingly find that you have become a bird in a fragrant forest filled with trees. What is your next move?

While flying through the forest, you stumble upon an empty branch that is perfect for building a nest on. Do you build a nest and shelter for the night or continue exploring?

By choosing to shelter for the night, you survived the dangers of the dark and lived to see another day.

While exploring the dark, you, unable to see where you are going, run into a tree branch and plummet to your death. As you close your eyes for the last time, you wish you had made a different choice.

While on your search to find the reason that you became a bird, you come across a very hungry bear. Do you attack the bear or try and run away?

You attack the bear, but its sheer strength allows it to beat you easily. As it prepares its final blow, you close your eyes and wish you had made a different choice.

Choosing to run away, you quickly flap your wings and soar to the highest tree you can find, surviving to see another day.
